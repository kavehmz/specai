# verdicts/ — verification records for specai itself

The durable committed home of the skill's own verification records (`verification_core.md` §6
rule 4). Records, not specs — nothing binding lives here. The whole directory is excluded from
eval runs (`reference/exemplars.md` §3). Run history and countersigns: `NOTES.md`.

- `taxonomy-seed.md` — the cross-run defect-pattern accumulation point (`verification_core.md`
  §2 rule 1's mechanical transform; source records cited by bare filename).
- `2026-07-17-standards-engagement/` — the founding standards-panel engagement: the panel record
  (consolidated findings + per-round resolution logs), the D1–D6 operator-decision packet, and
  the verbatim raw reports of blind re-verification rounds 2–6 (four lenses each; round-1 raw
  reports were not preserved — disclosed in the panel record).
- `2026-07-17-postseal-audit/` — the operator-invited post-seal audit over `630ac46`: the
  compiled record + four verbatim raw lens reports.

Records are grouped one subdirectory per engagement; a compiled record cites its raw reports by
bare filename and lives beside them. Raw reports are preserved verbatim and never edited
(record-fix corrections, when they happen, are logged in `NOTES.md`'s Exempted edits).
